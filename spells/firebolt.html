Fire Bolt

evocation cantrip

Range 240ft (48sq)
Targets 1 / 2
Attack Ranged
Damage 2d10 fire

Damage

Min2 Low9 Avg11 High14 Max20
2d10 TypeFire AttackRanged SorceryEmpowered, Twinned

A mote of fire flies from your hand.

Make a ranged spell attack agains a creature or object within range. On a hit, the target takes 2d10 fire damage. A flammable object hit by this spell ignites if it isn’t being worn or carried.

Player’s Handbook – page 242

This cantrip’s damage increases by 1d10 when you reach 5th, 11th, and 17th levels.

This spell can be twinned. PHB 102

The range of this spell is doubled for you, and your targets gain no benefit from cover. PHB 170
