Friends

enchantment cantrip

Range Self
Targets 1
Duration 1 minute (conc.)

You discreetly straighten your outfit as a smile spreads across your target’s face.

For the duration, you have advantage on all Charisma checks directed at one creature of your choice that isn’t hostile toward you. When the spell ends, the creature realizes that you used magic to influence its mood and becomes hostile toward you. A creature prone to violence might attack you. Another creature might seek retibution in other ways (at the DM’s discretion), depending on the nature of your interaction with it.

Player’s Handbook – page 244

This spell is cast on you, not the target, and cannot be twinned. PHB 102
