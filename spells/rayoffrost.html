Ray of Frost

evocation cantrip

Range 120ft (24sq)
Targets 1 / 2
Attack Ranged
Damage 2d8 cold

2d8 TypeCold AttackRanged EffectSlowed by 10ft SorceryEmpowered, Twinned

A frigid beam of blue-white light streaks from your hand.

Make a ranged spell attack against a creature within range. On a hit, it takes 2d8 cold damage, and its speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn.

Player’s Handbook – page 271

This cantrip’s damage increases by 1d8 when you reach 5th, 11th, and 17th levels.

This spell can be twinned. PHB 102

The range of this spell is doubled for you, and your targets gain no benefit from cover. PHB 170
