You cause a tremor in the ground within range. Each creature other than you in that area must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 1d6 bludgeoning damage and is knocked prone. If the ground in that area is loose earth or stone, it becomes difficult terrain until cleared, with each 5-foot-diameter portion requiring at least 1 minute to clear by hand.

At Higher Levels The damage increases by 1d6 for each level above 1st.

Elemental Evil Companion – page 17

This spell was granted by elemental forces unknown. It doesn’t count against your known spells, can’t be replaced, and doesn’t cause wild magic surges.

This spell has been modified by later resources. The space affected has changed from a 10-foot radius sphere to 10 feet from the caster. EEC 17, Crawford

All the ground in the spell’s area becomes difficult terrain if eligible. Crawford

A creature takes no damage if it saves, even at higher levels.
