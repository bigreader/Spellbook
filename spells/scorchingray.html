Several fiery bolts arc toward your targets in quick succession.

You hurl three rays of fire toward one or several targets in range. Make a ranged spell attack for each ray. On each hit, the target takes 2d6 fire damage.

At Higher Levels The number of rays increases by 1 for each level above 2nd.

Player’s Handbook – page 273

The range of this spell is doubled for you, and your targets gain no benefit from cover. PHB 170

A sorcerer’s Empowered Spell could affect more than one ray of a scorching ray, abiding by the feature’s die limit. For instance, if you create three rays with the spell and you have a +3 Charisma modifier, you could reroll one of the damage dice for each ray, or two of the damage dice for one ray and one of the damage dice for another one. SAGE v2.2 p5

Even though the duration of this spell is instantaneous, you choose the targets and resolve the attacks consecutively, not all at once. If you want, you can declare all your targets before making any attacks, but you would still roll separately for each attack (and damage, if appropriate). SAGE v2.2 p12

This spell doesn’t ignite objects.
