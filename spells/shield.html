Shield

first level abjuration

Time Reaction
Duration 1 round

An invisible barrier of magical force deflects the attack.

You can cast this spell as a reaction when you are hit by an attack or targeted by the magic missile spell. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack, and you take no damage from magic missile.
