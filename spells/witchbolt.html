Witch Bolt

first level evocation

Range 60ft (12sq)
Targets 1 / 2
Duration 1 minute (conc.)
Attack Ranged
Damage 1d12 lightning

Damage

Min1 Low4 Avg7 High10 Max12
1d12 TypeLightning AttackRanged SorceryEmpowered, Twinned

A beam of crackling blue energy lances out toward your target, forming a sustained arc of lightning.

Make a ranged spell attack against a creature within range. On a hit, the target takes 1d12 lightning damage, and on each of your turns for the duration, you can use your action to deal 1d12 lightning damage to the target automatically. The spell ends if you use your action to do anything else. The spell also ends if the target is ever outside the spell’s range or if it has total cover from you.

At Higher Levels The initial damage increases by 1d12 for each level above 1st.

Player’s Handbook – page 289

This spell can be twinned at all levels. PHB 102

The range of this spell is doubled for you, and your targets gain no benefit from cover. PHB 170
